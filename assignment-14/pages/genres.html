    Fiction

    Fiction books encompass imaginative or fabricated stories that may not necessarily be based on real events. This genre includes novels, short stories, and novellas that can transport readers to different worlds, times, or experiences.

    Fantasy

    Fantasy books feature elements of magic, mythical creatures, or supernatural phenomena. These stories often take place in fictional worlds and involve adventures beyond the boundaries of reality.

    Mystery

    Mystery books revolve around the solving of a crime, puzzle, or enigmatic event. They typically involve detectives, amateur sleuths, or ordinary individuals uncovering hidden truths through investigation and deduction.

    Romance

    Romance books focus on relationships, love, and emotional connections between characters. These stories often explore themes of passion, intimacy, and the complexities of human emotions.
