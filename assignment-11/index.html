Fall in Montana

Montana experiences distinct seasons, including fall, which is characterized by changing foliage, cooler temperatures, and a crisp atmosphere. Fall in Montana typically spans from September to November.

One of the highlights of fall is the changing colors of the foliage. The state's diverse landscapes, including mountains, forests, and plains, showcase vibrant hues of (e.g., red, orange, and yellow) leaves.
